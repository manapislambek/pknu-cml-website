Welcome to Our Laboratory

We are located at the beautiful ocean area in Nam-gu, Busan, South Korea. The research specialty of our group is in the area of architectural and construction materials, including cement chemistry, early age set and stiffening, rheological behavior, durability, recycling of industrial waste, radioactive waste separation and immobilization, development of advanced construction materials (e.g. use of CNT, graphene, or other nano-materials), etc.

Please check our past and current on-going research activities. Potential applicants are always welcome to contact the group member for the questions and the application.
